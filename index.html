About Us

Envision safeguarding your prized content using a universally accepted technology, valid across the globe

True technology resonates with reality. Guided by this principle, we endeavor to seamlessly integrate technology into everyday life.

Traditionally, safeguarding our vital documents, be it artistic works, agreements, or other essential records, has required notarization worldwide. This process isn't just costly but also time-consuming. Authenticating a document registered in one country for validity in another often entails navigating a maze of red tape. Moreover, verifying the legitimacy of a presented document poses its own set of challenges.Enter BitInkChain. With our platform, you can immediately sign and record any document, embedding it with a timestamp via cutting-edge blockchain technology. Plus, with our efficient verification service, you can swiftly access and authenticate any document you've signed and registered. The future of document security and verification is here.
